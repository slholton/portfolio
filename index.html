About Shavonne

future developer | creative director

Shavonne Holton was born and raised in Indianapolis and has loved the arts since the age of four. In 2017, she expressed her creativity by building websites on Squarespace. Her intention with each website was to highlight untapped innovations, streamline e-commerce solutions, and curate engaging content.

Once she realized what she enjoyed was creative directing, Shavonne wanted to understand the "tech" behind her art and enrolled in the Web Development Program at Eleven Fifty Academy. Now, Shavonne aspires to do Front End Development for a brand marketing agency or streaming service and learn more about UX/UI.
